The Problem

Breaking into public service is harder than it should be

Despite millions of government roles, there's no single place to find them — and no clear playbook for how to get hired.

Fragmented Listings

Jobs are scattered across USAJobs, state portals, city websites, agency pages, and fellowship programs. No single search covers them all.

Unclear Timelines

Federal hiring can take months. Applications disappear into a black hole. Candidates are left guessing at every stage of the process.

Insider Knowledge

The students who land government roles often have connections and information that isn't publicly available. That shouldn't be a requirement.

"Government hiring is a black box. I want to work in government, but I don't even know where to start."

— Grad student, public policy program
How PublicPath Helps

One place. Every level. Real guidance.

We do the work of finding, filtering, and explaining government opportunities so you can focus on applying.

01

Centralized Job Board

Browse aggregated listings from federal agencies, state governments, city portals, and fellowship programs — all in one searchable interface with filters for location, function area, and government level.

02

Weekly Curated Digest

Every week, we hand-pick the best roles and deliver them to your inbox — including opportunities shared directly by people working in the field that you won't find on any job board.

03

Hiring Process Guidance

Understand federal resumes, GS scales, hiring timelines, clearance processes, and what makes a strong public sector application — without needing insider connections to figure it out.

04

Network Access

Get roles sourced from our founder's network of government insiders, political operatives, and public service leaders — the kind of leads that usually only circulate privately.
