07  /  About PublicPath

Why PublicPath exists.

"Breaking into government shouldn't depend on who you know. But too often, it does."

Every year, thousands of talented students want to work in government. Many of them never make it. Not because they aren't qualified, but because the path isn't clear. Roles are hard to find, the process is difficult to navigate, and expectations aren't transparent.

PublicPath was founded to fix that.

Our founder, Hira Qureshi, spent nearly a decade working in federal government, including roles at the U.S. Treasury Department and on the Biden-Harris campaign, where she built talent pipelines and placed people into public service careers. She got her own start with no formal network or inside connections.

That experience, combined with years of seeing qualified candidates shut out by gaps in information and access, is what PublicPath is built to solve.

What we believe

Government works best when it reflects the people it serves. Talented, mission-driven candidates exist everywhere: in every school, every city. What's missing isn't talent. It's access.

We're a nonprofit platform with a simple mission: to help the next generation of talent access public service careers, regardless of where they went to school or who they know.
