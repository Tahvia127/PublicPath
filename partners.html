Universities

Help your students find careers in government

Career services offices work hard to connect students to opportunities, but government careers are notoriously difficult to navigate. Most offices don't have the specialized knowledge to guide students through the federal hiring process.

PublicPath fills that gap. We aggregate hard-to-find government roles across federal, state, and local government, filtered for early-career candidates and paired with plain-English guidance on eligibility and next steps.

  • Curated job listings filtered for early-career candidates — no noise
  • Plain-English guides on federal resumes, timelines, and special hiring paths
  • Weekly digest your students can subscribe to directly
  • Free for your students and your office — always

Fellowships & Civic Orgs

Put your program in front of the right candidates

Fellowship programs and civic organizations are some of the most important on-ramps into public service — but too many qualified candidates never find them. Awareness is a persistent challenge across the sector.

PublicPath surfaces fellowships alongside traditional government roles, putting your program in front of motivated, early-career candidates actively looking for a way in.

  • Featured placement alongside curated government job listings
  • Candidates arrive pre-informed about your application process and timeline
  • Reach people who have self-selected into public service — not generic job seekers
  • Works for paid fellowships, leadership programs, and civic initiatives

Agencies & Employers

Get your open roles in front of candidates who are ready

PublicPath works with government agencies, campaigns, and civic organizations that are actively hiring early-career talent. Our audience has already self-selected into public service — they're not browsing generic job boards.

We're especially effective for roles that struggle to gain visibility through traditional channels: entry-level policy positions, program coordinators, communications roles, and fellowships.

  • Featured in the weekly digest sent to our subscriber base
  • "From our network" section spotlights roles flagged by our contacts
  • Ideal for entry-level and early-career roles that need targeted reach
