For Universities

For universities and career services offices

Career services offices work hard to connect students to opportunities, but government careers are notoriously difficult to navigate, and most offices don't have the specialized knowledge to guide students through the process.

PublicPath is designed to fill that gap. We aggregate hard-to-find government roles, explain what they actually require, and give students a clear path from interest to application, without requiring insider knowledge or connections.

As a university partner, your students get early access to curated opportunities across federal, state, and local government, filtered for early-career candidates and accompanied by plain-English guidance on eligibility and next steps.

There's no cost to your students or your office. We're in early development and actively building our founding university partnerships. If you work with students who are interested in public service, we'd love to connect.

For Fellowships & Civic Orgs

For fellowship programs and civic organizations

Fellowship programs and civic organizations are some of the most important on-ramps into public service, but too many qualified candidates never find them. Awareness is a persistent challenge across the sector.

PublicPath surfaces fellowships and civic opportunities alongside traditional government roles, putting your program in front of motivated, early-career candidates who are actively looking for a way into public service.

We also help candidates understand what fellowships like yours actually involve: the application process, timeline expectations, and what a strong candidate looks like. The people who reach you will be better prepared and more likely to succeed.

Whether you run a paid fellowship, a leadership development program, or a civic engagement initiative, we'd like to understand what you're building and explore how we can help expand your reach to the candidates who need you most.

For Employers & Agencies

Government agencies and civic employers

PublicPath works with government agencies, campaigns, and civic organizations that are actively hiring early-career talent. We surface your open roles to a curated audience of motivated candidates who have already self-selected into public service careers.

Our platform is especially effective for roles that are hard to fill through traditional channels: entry-level policy positions, program coordinators, digital communications roles, and fellowships that don't get the visibility they deserve on generic job boards.

We also maintain a "From our network" section in our weekly digest — a curated list of opportunities shared by contacts in the field. If you have roles you'd like us to flag for our pilot cohort, reach out.
